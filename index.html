Our Story

Lab results should not leave people confused.

Many people only seek care after pain, fatigue, or visible symptoms. But risks such as prediabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol, anemia, kidney risk, and liver risk can develop silently for years.

Even when people get tested, the experience often ends with a PDF full of numbers, reference ranges, and medical terms. ANTES Health closes the gap between testing and action by making preventive health easier to understand and easier to follow over time.

The gap we solve

Preventive care is fragmented.

Current options often sell tests, but they do not guide people through the full preventive journey.

01

Generic checkups

People are offered broad lab packages that may not match their age, family history, or risk factors.

02

Confusing results

Reports show numbers and reference ranges, but little context about what matters and why.

03

No follow-up

A single result rarely changes behavior. People need reminders, trends, and responsible next steps.

Our Approach

From lab numbers to a preventive health journey.

ANTES Health connects risk assessment, appropriate testing, clear interpretation, longitudinal tracking, and follow-up in one simple experience.

1

Risk assessment

Users answer questions about age, family history, habits, previous results, and cardiometabolic risks.

2

Personalized panel

We recommend preventive tests that fit the user profile instead of pushing unnecessary packages.

3

Laboratory testing

Users complete testing through collaborating or partner laboratories.

4

Clear report

We transform lab values into visual explanations, priorities, and trend-based insights.

5

Follow-up

Users receive reminders, education, and guidance on when to repeat tests or consult a professional.
