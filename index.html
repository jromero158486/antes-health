The problem

The gap is not only testing. It is what happens before and after testing.

Many people only check their health when symptoms appear. But risks such as prediabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol, anemia, and cardiometabolic disease can develop silently for years.

Even when people get lab results, they often receive a PDF full of numbers without clear context, trends, or next steps. ANTES Health turns that confusion into a guided preventive journey.

The solution

From lab numbers to preventive action.

We connect the steps that are usually fragmented: risk, testing, understanding, follow-up, and referral when needed.

Our Story

Why ANTES Health exists

ANTES Health was born from a simple observation: people do not need more confusing health information; they need clearer, earlier, and more responsible guidance.

In many families, lab results arrive as numbers on a PDF, but the real question remains unanswered: “What should I do next?”

Our Approach

Risk-based, understandable, and continuous prevention.

ANTES Health does not start with a generic lab package. We start with the person: age, family history, habits, previous results, and risk factors. Then we connect testing, understanding, and follow-up into one preventive journey.

A prevention journey built on evidence

Our MVP is guided by preventive screening recommendations, health literacy principles, and responsible digital health design.

01

Screening should be risk-aware

Hypertension screening is recommended for adults, with confirmation outside the clinical setting before treatment.

 02

Prevention needs next steps

Prediabetes screening guidance emphasizes referral to effective preventive interventions when risk is found.

 03

Abnormal results need confirmation

Kidney guidelines emphasize GFR and albuminuria for people at risk, plus repeat testing to confirm abnormal findings.

 04

AI must be responsible

AI can support communication and workflows, but health products need governance, human oversight, privacy, and safety.

Data flow

From lab results to action.

Fragmented health data becomes a clear preventive journey for the person, family, and clinician when appropriate.

Inputs such as profile, family history, habits, previous results, and lab results flow into the ANTES Health prevention layer, producing a clear report, reminders, suggested next steps, doctor-ready summaries, and family views with consent.

What we do

  • Recommend preventive tests based on risk profile.
  • Transform lab results into understandable explanations.
  • Track markers over time.
  • Send reminders and follow-up prompts.
  • Suggest responsible next steps.
  • Refer to healthcare professionals when needed.

What we do not do

  • We do not replace physicians.
  • We do not provide emergency care.
  • We do not diagnose automatically.
  • We do not sell unnecessary testing.
  • We do not share health data without consent.

MVP Pilot

Our first supervised MVP pilot.

We are preparing a supervised MVP pilot with 30–50 adults aged 30–55 in Lima, Peru, focused on cardiometabolic prevention.

Join the pilot waitlist

Who

Adults 30–55 with family history of diabetes, hypertension risk, high cholesterol, overweight, or sedentary lifestyle.

What participants receive

Risk assessment, suggested lab panel, clear report, 12-week WhatsApp follow-up, education, and referral guidance.

What we measure

Assessment completion, testing conversion, understanding, engagement, willingness to pay, next-step actions, and satisfaction/NPS.

FAQ

Questions people should ask before trusting a health product.

Does ANTES Health replace a doctor?

No. ANTES Health supports prevention, education, follow-up, and referral. It does not replace medical care.

Is ANTES Health available now?

We are preparing our first supervised MVP pilot.

Who is the first pilot for?

Adults aged 30–55 in Lima with cardiometabolic risk factors.

What tests are included?

The first focus is cardiometabolic health, including markers such as blood pressure, glucose/HbA1c, lipids, kidney markers, and other tests only when appropriate.

How do you protect health data?

We are designing the platform around consent, privacy, and user control.

Can families use it together?

Yes, the long-term vision includes family-centered prevention with consent and privacy controls.
