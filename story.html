Our Story

Why ANTES Health exists.

People do not need more confusing health information. They need clearer, earlier, and more responsible guidance.

The gap we saw

In many families, lab results arrive as numbers on a PDF, but the real question remains unanswered: “What should I do next?”

The gap is not only access to testing. It is choosing the right tests, understanding the results, noticing trends, and following up before symptoms appear.

ANTES means before.

  • Before symptoms.
  • Before complications.
  • Before high medical costs.
  • Before families feel they are too late.

Founder

Built by a biomedical engineer from Peru.

ANTES Health was founded by Joselyn Romero Avila, a Peruvian Biomedical Engineering student working at the intersection of medical AI, biosensing, medical imaging, and digital health.

Her goal is to make preventive care more understandable, continuous, and accessible, starting with cardiometabolic prevention.
