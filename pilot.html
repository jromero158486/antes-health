MVP Pilot

Testing the first preventive journey.

We are preparing a supervised MVP pilot with 30–50 adults aged 30–55 in Lima, Peru, focused on cardiometabolic prevention.

Join pilot waitlist

Who

Adults aged 30–55 with cardiometabolic risk factors.

  • Family history of diabetes
  • Hypertension risk
  • High cholesterol
  • Overweight
  • Sedentary lifestyle

What participants receive

  • Preventive health assessment
  • Suggested lab panel
  • Clear preventive report
  • 12-week WhatsApp follow-up
  • Referral guidance when needed

What we measure

  • Assessment completion
  • Lab testing conversion
  • User understanding
  • Follow-up engagement
  • Willingness to pay
  • Responsible next-step actions

MVP artifact

Participants will receive a clear report, not only raw values.
