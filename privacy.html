Privacy & Data Protection

Health data is sensitive. ANTES Health is being designed around consent, privacy, user control, and responsible handling of preventive health information.

Working principles

  • We will only collect health information with user consent.
  • Users should control what is shared and with whom.
  • Family features require explicit consent and privacy controls.
  • Data protection processes will be expanded before the MVP launch.
  • This page is an early-stage placeholder and not a final privacy policy.
