Privacy & Data Protection

Health data deserves trust by design.

This is an early placeholder and will be expanded before MVP launch.

Health data is sensitive. ANTES Health will only collect data with consent and will design user controls around what information is shared and with whom.

Family features will require explicit consent. Users should be able to decide whether a family member, clinician, laboratory, or partner can access any information.

This page is not a final privacy policy. It is a public placeholder describing the principles that will guide the MVP.
